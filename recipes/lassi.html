Mango Lassi:

Description:

Mango Lassi is a popular drink all over the Indian Subcontinent. It is really refreshing on hot summer days.

It is found all over the world but Indian Subcontinent one is different from others.

It is made of ripe mango, yoghurt, sugar etc. and its yellow in color.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups plain whole milk yogurt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 mangoes - peeled, seeded, and chopped
  • 4 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cardamom

Steps:

  1. Place the yogurt, milk, mangoes, white sugar, and cardamom into the jar of a blender and blend until smooth, about 2 minutes.
  2. Chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour or until cold, and serve sprinkled with a little ground cardamom.

Nutrition:

  • 195 calories; protein 6.8g; carbohydrates 31.9g; fat 5.5g; cholesterol 20.8mg; sodium 83.6mg (approx)
